Detailed Description

template<typename... Ts, typename... Us>
struct DIRE_NS::ParameterExtractor< std::tuple< Metadata< Ts... >, Us... > >

A class that is used to extract a tuple from the arguments passed to the constructor of a property. Version for when the parameter list includes Metadata. It's going to strip the first type of the tuple, isolating the Metadata, then repack the tuple with the rest of the arguments.
